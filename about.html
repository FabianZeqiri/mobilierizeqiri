Oak Wood for our forniture

Oak wood is renowned for its durability, timeless appeal, and strength. It's perfect for crafting heirloom-quality pieces that can last for generations. It can be sanded and refinished, ensuring long-term usability.

Steel for our forniture

Steel is known for its strength, modern aesthetic, and resistance to wear and tear. It's often used in contemporary designs and industrial-style furniture. Steel, for example, can be heavy-duty, while aluminum is lighter and rust-resistant.

Faux leather for our forniture

Leather is luxurious, durable, and ages beautifully over time. Full-grain leather, the highest quality, develops a patina, making it even more appealing as it ages. Faux leather offers a vegan option with a similar look and feel.
